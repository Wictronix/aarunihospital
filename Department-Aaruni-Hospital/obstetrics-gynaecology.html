Obstetrics & Gynaecology

At Aaruni Multispeciality Hospital, we place a strong focus on delivering high-quality gynaecological care since we recognise that women of all ages have varied health concerns. The Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology strives to deliver the most complete women's healthcare services available.

Aaruni Multispeciality Hospital guarantees that difficult clinical circumstances are handled with simplicity and skill by using qualified personnel, cutting-edge technology and equipment, and highly experienced gynaecologists. Our obstetrics department guarantees the greatest pregnancy treatment, from conception to post-natal care.

The Aaruni Multispeciality Facility is one of the most well-equipped obstetrics and gynaecology centres in Jhunjhunu, offering the complete range of women's healthcare services, making it a highly sought-after maternity hospital. The following are the standout features:

Providing the best pregnancy care with renowned consultants who specialise in dealing with high-risk pregnancies and pregnancy medical disorders. Endocrine (Diabetes, Thyroid, etc.), Neurology, and Haematology clinic for pregnant women:

  • Electronic Fetal Monitoring in Labour for safety of the baby
  • TCustomized LDRP (Labour, Delivery, Recovery, and Postpartum) Suites Electronic Fetal Monitoring in Labor for Baby Safety
  • Regular prenatal lessons for expectant women to care for their unborn child
  • Dietary recommendations for all pregnant and breastfeeding mothers
  • Normal birth painless labour facility
  • Subspecialties in Gynaecology
  • Folliculometry Screening for Infertility and IVF
  • Deformity correction
  • Onco-Gynaecology
