Director's Message

Aaruni Multispeciality Hospital

The authorized institution was established by eminent orthopaedist Dr. Sunil Poonia, MBBS, MS (Orthopedics) with the goal of offering the finest standards of medical care, clinical research, teaching, and training at an affordable cost.
The guiding values of Aaruni Multispeciality are to provide medical services to patients with care, compassion, and dedication.
The hospital, which is accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers (NABH), boasts a team of experienced doctors and trained employees, modern medical equipment, and state-of-the-art infrastructure facilities.
With perseverance, devotion, and compassion, Aaruni Multispeciality Hospital provides holistic healthcare services not only to city residents, but also to persons living in remote locations.

Health Tips

Health Tips about Food

  • We should limit the consumption of total and saturated fats (often coming from foods of animal origin), and completely avoid trans fats; reading the labels helps to identify the sources.
  • When cooking, we should boil, steam or bake, rather than frying, remove the fatty part of meat, use vegetable oils.
  • When shopping, we should choose products with lower sodium content.

Health Tips for Covid-19

  • Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds, especially after being in a public place, or after blowing your nose, coughing or sneezing.
  • Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces daily. This includes tables, doorknobs, light switches, countertops, handles, desks, phones, keyboards, toilets, faucets and sinks.
  • Wear a face mask that has the most protection that you'll wear and fits comfortably when near others or pets. Change the face mask each day.
