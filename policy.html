POLICY

The environment, as well as the wellbeing of its staff and patients, as well as their safety and social welfare, are all priorities for Aaruni Multispeciality Hospital. The Environmental, Health, Safety, and Social (EHSS) policy will reaffirm the hospital's commitment to ensuring ongoing improvement and, via its implementation, will control, reduce, or completely eliminate environmental and social risks and impacts for all current and future Hospital. To fulfil its promise, the hospital will:

  • Ensure that all Company activities adhere to all applicable local, state, and federal E&S laws, as well as accepted standards and best practises.
  • Manage healthcare activities in the safest possible way by identifying and evaluating risks, and by putting in place efficient controls for Occupational Health and Safety, Community Health and Safety, and Environmental Conservation and Promotion of Sustainable Environment Practices.
  • Encourage the efficient use of natural resources and guard against environmental pollution in all Hospital. This commitment will also be followed in the planning, creation, and management of future Hospital.
